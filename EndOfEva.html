EVANGELION

The End of Evangelion serves as an alternate ending to the polarizing final episodes of Neon Genesis Evangelion. The film covers the same events that transpired but from a more conventional perspective. With the fate of the universe hanging in the balance, the climactic final battle draws near. Still to this day continues to have an impact on film-making.

Compared to a conventional movie, End of Evangelion is more akin to art itself. Whether it's because of its presentation, production, or themes, it is truly mesmerizing. A work of art unlike anything seen before and after its time.

It is a commonly held belief that Hideaki Anno created End of Evangelion as a form of revenge against Evangelion fans unhappy with the existing ending to the TV series. In reality, the End of Evangelion is closer to the original concept for the series ending, which was changed due to budget and censorship issues.
